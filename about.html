About ASP Group

Since 1989, ASP Group has been a prominent player in the automotive industry, specializing in high-quality rubber products. We supply leading brands such as JCB, Volvo, Mahindra, TVS Auto, and more.

Our Vision

To be at the forefront of the industry by delivering excellence in innovation, technology, and sustainability.

Our Values

  • Quality in every step
  • Integrity in our dealings
  • Commitment to innovation
  • Focus on customer satisfaction

Expanding Capabilities

To create value for our community, shareholders, and customers through integrity, compassion, and learning.

Our Mission

  1. INTEGRITY: Build crystal clear relations with customers by delivering world-class products, quality, and processes.
  2. COMPASSION: Respect the physical and social environment and lead environmental improvement in the industry and society.
  3. LEARNING: Focus on innovation and continuous improvements from our past bad experiences and inculcate them into our daily work to create a more sustainable future.

OUR CERTIFICATIONS

ISO 9001 : Quality Management

ISO/TS 16949 : Automotive Quality

ISO 14001 : Environmental Management

ASP Group is committed to delivering high-quality products and services to our clients. Our quality systems are the result of extensive efforts to standardize inputs, processes, and outputs across the organization. With dedicated and experienced teams managing quality assurance, we ensure consistency throughout all operations. Many companies within the ASP Group are specialized OEM suppliers to the automotive industry, consistently meeting strict quality standards of less than 5 ppm to ensure the highest level of performance.
